Bitburger

PASSION AND COMMITMENT FOR A BETTER BEER

FOR 200 YEARS

For more than 200 years, Bitburger produces with maximum care and highest quality from the finest raw ingredients according to German Law of Purity.

 

Directly in our neighborhood, in Holsthum near Bitburg at the Eifel South Nature Park, Bitburger's Siegel barley grows, which together with the barley from Hallertau Bitburger gives Premium Pils its dry taste. Combined with our natural yeast, the valuable precious water from the Triasic valley and the best barley malt from the drink, we create the unmistakable qualities of Bitburg and the unique taste of our beer.

The most popular beer in Germany is served in more than 70 countries and over 50,000 bars, restaurants and pubs.

Regardless of the country, the popular slogan "Bitte ein Bit" is immediately noticeable. This slogan for beer lovers is inseparably linked to the unique quality, taste and unmistakable beer experience.

IDEL takes care that the unique quality of Bitburger arrives in your glasses.
