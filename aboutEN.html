Our Clients

The number of the clients satisfied with our services and products continues to grow daily.

CAPRICCIO BAR

Capriccio Bar is one of the oldest bars in town. Since 2001 they serve the highest quality beer. There you can find every beer IDEL offers, as either draft beer or in bottles.

 

BILLIONAIRE VILA

Billionaire Villa offers a relaxing, sophisticated, seductive and stunning atmosphere. Known for the nightlife there you can find cocktails, champagne, wine as well as IDEL's beers, served by qualified staff that imposes the feeling of being a true billionaire.

 

MEDUZA IRISH PUB

The Irish Pub & Restaurant Meduza in Tirana is an authentic Irish pub where you can enjoy the soft art of conversation, the Hard Rock music or a simple moment of reflection. At Irish Pub Meduza Irish dishes are accompanied by the excellent IDEL beers.

 

VILA ERA

ERA is and will remain for many years a leader in the field of food service. The first in this unique style of restaurants, the only one with the top quality of the catering service and uncompromising in the delivery service. Of course in Era restaurants you can also enjoy the beers that IDEL brings.

 

MUMA BISTRO BAR

Recently opened, Muma Bistro Bar has a loyal clientele created right away thanks to the atmosphere of warm furnishings, delicious food and a modern architecture that invites you to stay there for hours. The customer loyalty is also related with the beer offered by IDEL.

 

L'UVA LOUNGE

Latin American music and IDEL's excellent beers are the unrivaled mix of this bar in the center of Tirana.

 

TEMA BRITISH PUB

This typical British pub in the center of Tirana offers to its customers the various beer bars offered by IDEL, in the bottled version as well as draft beer.

 

TIKI TIRANA

Offering something of a castaway vibe, this two-floored shipwreck of a bar complete with wooden face masks and lampshades made from seashells is about as close as it currently gets to a cocktail bar in contemporary Tirana. As well as the almost compulsory Erdinger or Bitburger behind the bar, find a range of colourful mixed drinks and freshly squeezed fruit juice.
